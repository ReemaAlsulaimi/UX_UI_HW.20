The Way Home Case Study

Tools:Adobe XD, Figma, Google drive, and trello.




Project purpose

To enhance the overall user experience of “The Way Home”, a non-profit with the mission of ending chronic homelessness in Washington D.C.

User Research

With D.C. Area Resi-dents. Ages 34+*, who have had experience donating to non-profit organization. *Average age of D.C. resident = 34

To identify the challenges people face when donating to charitable organizations and to determine the factors that motivate them to donate. This information will inform the redesign of organization’s content portrayal, UI, and actionable steps.

With users, to further clarify what would incentivize people to donate to a non-profit related to homeless-ness.

- 83% would be more likely to donate if they knew how their donation focused on long-term impact. - 70% would be more likely to donate if they knew how their donation money was being allocated.

Solutions

Based on the user research, we decided that it would be most benificial to implemement the following solutions:

- Create clear call-to-action buttons to help users easily engage with the organization.

- Redesign the website’s interface and content, based on our 3 main key research insights, to make it more engaging and informative.
