U.S. DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE

Tools:Figma, Miro, google forms, and zoom

A Statement describing why the user visits the website

Noura Toldo is a 33-year-old married mother who wants to be an expert in Plant Breeding and to find organic foods for her kids. She enjoys planting flowers and tiny trees but needs more time to learn about Plant Breeding. Moreover, she cares about her kids' food and always wants to prevent Food-borne illnesses. Noura knows that the U.S. DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE (USDA) website is an excellent source of information to achieve her two goals. While visiting the USDA website, she finds excellent research on Plant Breeding. On the other hand, many sources about Child Nutrition Programs (FNS) and additional nutrition programs exist.

Problem Statement

The USDA Website navigation is difficult to maneuver in order for the typical user new to agriculture can follow.  This can lead to less traffic flow for the USDA and more complaints and phone calls for simple questions also The website does not feel its an agriculture website.
